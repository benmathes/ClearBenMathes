# CLEAR - Startup Equity Navigator

A standalone tool for understanding startup job offers, particularly the equity compensation components.

## What it does

CLEAR helps you analyze and understand:
- **Salary vs Equity tradeoffs** - Visualize the balance between cash and stock options
- **Stock option details** - Number of options, strike price, vesting schedules
- **Company valuation context** - What your equity percentage actually means
- **Equity worth scenarios** - Model potential outcomes at different company valuations
- **Key terms explained** - Early exercise, liquidation preference, exercise windows, and more

## Usage

Open `index.html` in a browser. Enter the details from your offer letter and explore the interactive analysis.

## Background

Originally built as part of a larger platform, this standalone version lets anyone analyze their startup offer without needing an account or sharing sensitive data.

## Tech Stack

- Backbone.js / Underscore.js for MVC structure
- Google Charts for visualizations
- Client-side only - no data leaves your browser
